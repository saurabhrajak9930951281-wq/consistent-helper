// Platform Capabilities

BUILT FOR
ENFORCEMENT

Every feature exists to eliminate the escape routes your past self used to justify quitting.

F.01 🔒
Locked Contracts
Goal parameters lock after first check-in. No editing frequency, deadline, or proof type retroactively. Your past self can't protect your future self from consequences.
F.02 ⚡
Server-Side Timer
Timer sessions are server-issued tokens. Start and stop are server-validated. Client cannot fake duration. The clock is ours, not yours.
F.03 💸
Financial Escrow
Real money. Real stakes. Funds held in escrow at goal creation. On failure: deducted immediately to a charity you cannot benefit from. No confirmation prompt.
F.04 👥
Accountability Partners
Assign partners who cannot dismiss failure notifications without acknowledging them. They see your streak, your misses, your goal name — automatically.
F.05 🌐
Public by Default
All active goals are publicly listed. Missed tasks appear as red entries — not hidden, not deleted. Streak history is immutable. No soft-resets, ever.
F.06 🤖
AI Excuse Detection
Submit an explanation for a miss. NLP classifier scores validity. Only externally verified reasons (e.g., hospital upload) can pause a goal. "I was tired" is auto-rejected.
F.07 📈
Dynamic Difficulty
After a 7-day streak, the system proposes harder goals: +10% duration, +1 frequency. Declining is logged and visible on your public profile.
F.08 📅
Calendar Lock
Goal tasks block calendar slots via Google Calendar API. Declining the calendar event is a pre-failure flag. Commitment starts before the day begins.
F.09 🏆
Leaderboard
Ranked by 30-day completion rate — not streak alone. Gaming with easy goals doesn't work. Category filters: fitness, study, coding, finance.

THE LAST
HOURS

Reminders are informational only. They do not extend deadlines. No snooze. No reschedule. Reminder seen = deadline acknowledged.

T−2h
First Reminder — Push + Email
Standard alert sent. Goal name, deadline time, proof type required. Two hours remains. This is informational only — the deadline does not move.
T−30m
Urgent Warning
"You have 30 minutes. No proof submitted." Sent to device and email. Partners may also receive a preview alert depending on accountability settings.
⚠ Urgency escalated
T−5m
Final Alert — Last Warning
"Final warning. Failure recorded in 5 minutes." No confirmation prompt. No button to delay. This is the last notification before automatic consequences trigger.
🔴 Critical — no more warnings after this
T+0
Task Expires — Consequence Logic Fires
Server cron marks task FAILED. Escrow deduction triggers. Partner notifications sent. Public failure post queued if miss count threshold met. No human intervention required.
